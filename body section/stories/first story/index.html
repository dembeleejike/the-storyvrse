The Siege of Avaris

When the northern provinces fractured under political betrayal, a single battle reshaped the continent.

Chapter 1: The Fracture

The northern provinces were no longer united. Betrayal ran through the halls of power, and alliances shattered overnight. The tension built as armies prepared to march.

Chapter 2: The Battle Begins

At dawn, the first siege engines rolled toward the walls of Avaris. Archers fired volleys, and the clash of swords echoed across the plains. Heroes and villains alike were revealed in the chaos.

Chapter 3: Aftermath

When the dust settled, the continent had changed forever. Borders were redrawn, leaders fallen, and legends born. The Siege of Avaris would be remembered for generations to come.
