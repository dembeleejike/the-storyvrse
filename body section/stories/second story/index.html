Rise of the Shadows

An uprising threatens the balance of power across the realms.

Whispers spread through the kingdoms. Secret factions formed alliances in the dark, plotting to overthrow the rulers.

Chapter 2: The Uprising

Rebellions erupted across key provinces. Citizens and soldiers alike faced the rising shadow of chaos, testing loyalties and courage.
